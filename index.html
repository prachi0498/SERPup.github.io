About Us

Who is SERPup

The Company started its journey in year 2018, where 5 friends from different sectors combined and merged their ideas to explore the new limits in field of analytics. They all brought their expertise on one page which eventually brought a concept of incorporating SEO and Data Analytics for building new strategies and helping other business grow. We are not only a cloud based Internet marketing platform but we also assist you in dealing with online marketing promotion like ways of increasing rating and reviews of your website, website traffic management etc. SERPup is presently associated with almost 140+ companies and everyday working with utmost professionalism and precision to bring new ideas and outcomes in the desired direction by molding the data according to business need.

  • Rank Tracking

    Rank Tracker provides you with accurate rankings of your website in leading search engines. The rankings can be tracked across multiple geographies including city based results and loaded with other additional features.

  • Competitors Analysis

    Competitors Analysis helps you in identifying your fiercest competitors, the ads they are running and the keywords they are ranking on. In addition, it displays data related to your competitor’s online presence and suggests the domains performance matrices.

    Examine your business alongside the dominant contemporaries of your niche based on:

    Target Location Keyword Search volume Keywords Categories SERP featured snippets Referring Domains Speed

  • Site Auditor

    The Site Auditor is one of the most important modules that increase your site’s indexing capabilities for the search engine. Site Auditor analyzes every aspect of your website and assists you in locating all the issues preventing your domain from securing top SERP rankings.

    Issues that we look upon:

    Efficient crawling by the bot Micro-Level Analysis Fixing of issues based on priority Efficiency of website Monitoring backlinks Site score and Many More

  • Backlinks Analysis

    Backlinks Analysis takes no time to extract a website's inbound link data and help you prepare an effective link building strategy by bifurcating the backlinks on it various properties. Activate the domain over our Link Center to get regular updates on its backlinks.

    Regular monitoring backlinks Active Links Lost links Referring Domains Anchor Text Authors filter
